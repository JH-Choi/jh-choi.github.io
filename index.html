Jaehoon Choi

I am a research intern at NAVER LABS, where I work on computer vision and machine learning.

At NAVERLABS I've worked on Lens Blur, HDR+, Jump, Portrait Mode, and Glass. I did my PhD at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik and funded by the NSF GRFP. I did my bachelors at the University of Toronto. I've received the C.V. Ramamoorthy Distinguished Research Award and the PAMI Young Researcher Award.
